# Exit Criteria for Moody’s Data Estate – Phase 2

**Milestone 1 – Project Kick‑off, Discovery & Project/Implementation Plan**
- Signed project charter, discovery report, implementation plan.
- 100 % resource commitment and approved budget.
- No outstanding scope‑change requests.
- Passes 5‑day Initial Review Period (plus up to 2 subsequent periods).

**Milestone 2 – Establish CI/CD and Deployment Automation**
- Fully configured pipelines, runbooks signed off.
- 100 % pass rate on all tests; no critical defects.
- Meets review periods.

**Milestone 3 – Develop and Deploy Ingestion Pipelines**
- .txt/.csv/.pdf ingest patterns deployed per client.
- 454 target files processed successfully; ≥ 90 % test coverage.
- Passes review periods.

**Milestone 4 – Embed Validation and Decommission**
- Validation logic embedded, legacy workflows retired.
- No defects impeding production.
- Meets review periods.

**Milestone 5 – Configure Data Quality and Final Integration/Optimizations**
- Production Kafka ingestion, CDS, automated data‑quality checks, monitoring.
- Performance benchmarks met.
- Meets review periods.

**Milestone 6 – Execute Full‑Scale Data Processing & Final Documentation**
- Full‑scale run confirms stability; documentation signed off.
- No outstanding change orders or high‑severity defects.
- Meets review periods.

---

**General Acceptance Criteria**
- Deliverables must be signed by authorized reps of both parties.
- Review periods: 5 business days initial, 3 business days first subsequent, 2 business days second subsequent.
- No outstanding change orders before invoicing.
