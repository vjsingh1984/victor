# Creating Custom Workflows for Victor AI

Comprehensive guide to creating custom workflows for Victor AI.

---

## Quick Summary

This guide covers creating custom workflows in Victor AI:
- **YAML Workflows**: Declarative, easy to read and modify
- **Python Workflows**: Programmatic, full flexibility
- **StateGraph Workflows**: LangGraph-compatible, complex state management
- **HITL Workflows**: Human-in-the-loop, interactive workflows
- **Advanced Features**: Parallel execution, conditional branching, error handling
- **Testing & Best Practices**: Unit testing, integration testing, workflow patterns

## Why Create Custom Workflows?

- **Automation**: Automate repetitive multi-step tasks
- **Consistency**: Ensure processes are followed consistently
- **Efficiency**: Combine multiple operations efficiently
- **Reusability**: Share workflows across teams
- **Maintainability**: Declarative workflows are easier to maintain
